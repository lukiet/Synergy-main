About Us

On May 30, 2020 at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, a group of friends came together to do their little bit for the community that was struggling to survive. The coming together of these friends gave birth to Synergy. Synergy is a community based charitable organization that focuses on empowering young school-going boys and girls. What started as a menstrual hygiene support program for teenage school-going girls has over the years transformed into a community empowerment organization that not only supports, on a regular basis, vulnerable families with hygiene items (toiletries and sanitary towels), but also sponsors children to school.

.As Synergy, we provide girls in our program with sanitary towels and toiletries. We provide school items including uniforms and stationery to both boys and girls, and support their families on a quarterly basis with food items.Our resource mobilization model is based on monthly contributions from members of the organization and biannual funds drive. Through the monthly contribution and the fund drives, we are able to sustain our programs and support children from vulnerable families from within Juja.

Our recent team-building event at Synergy was a powerful experience that brought us closer together as a group. Quarterly food distribution

This quarter, Synergy came together once again for our regular food distribution event, and it was an incredibly fulfilling experience. As a group, we worked diligently to organize, pack, and distribute essential food items to those in need within our community. The teamwork and dedication of every member were evident as we ensured that everything ran smoothly and efficiently. Each interaction with the individuals and families we served reminded us of the importance of our mission. By the end of the day, we were filled with a deep sense of purpose, knowing that our collective efforts had made a real difference in the lives of many.
